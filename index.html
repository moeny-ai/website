what?

Welcome to moeny.ai, where New York’s AI experts are coming together to explore what AI can be. On 10/24/2024, we attended a ConnectHV Networking Event in Hudson, NY, and were inspired by the incredible mixture of experts in the room. That experience sparked a simple yet powerful idea: to bring top people together and make AI both accessible and precision-focused.

From there, we set out to build moeny, a network and platform centered on scalability, impact, and simplicity. Inspired by the "Mixture of Experts" (MoE) concept, the bold direction of tinycorp, and George Hotz’s approach to building products and teams, we’re creating a team of AI pros who value performance without unnecessary frills.

As we grow, we’re open to building moeny as a community-driven organization, potentially using a DAO model to directly involve contributors in decision-making. For now, our focus is clear: AI that’s lean, efficient, and built to solve real-world problems.

jobs?

We’re bootstrapping, so we’re not hiring full-time engineers just yet. However, we’ll be posting bounties soon and are open to ambitious interns who share our vision of no-frills, high-performance AI.

Interested? Keep an eye out for our upcoming bounty page. Completing bounties will allow you to earn while getting a feel for our work and culture.

Note: Once funding is obtained and we begin hiring, only applicants who have completed at least one bounty with moeny will be considered.

huh?

How can I get the moeny team involved in my ML project?
We’re excited to collaborate with those tackling real-world challenges through machine learning. If you're looking for a team to help simplify complex AI processes, optimize performance, or bring fresh insights to your ML project, we’re interested in connecting.
Where’s moeny development happening?
On GitHub and on our self hosted RocketChat – jump in if you want to see what we’re working on. Stop by our office to see and pet the server running the RocketChat Deployment.
How can moeny work for me?
If you’re interested in a custom AI solution or partnership, reach out to us on RocketChat.
How can I work for moeny?
Refer to jobs above. Contributions on GitHub will be a great first step. Stay tuned for more information.
Can I invest in moeny.ai?
We are interested in angel investment at this time. Your contributions and PRs are also another great way to invest.
What’s the goal of moeny.ai?
It's still very early in our journey and our goals are still taking shape. Read what above.
